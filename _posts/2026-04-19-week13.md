In this week we talked about open projects that are not necessarily software products. I chose Internet Archive since I  quite like the idea of Internet Archive and Wayback Machine. As a music history lover, I often use Internet Archive to find composer manuscript, old biography and other historical documents. Although still count as a software, My favorite open project is eBird. As a daily eBird user I frequently upload my observations and browse other's observation for my own reference. I appreciate the idea of citizen science very much. OpenLitterMap is also quite interesting to me, and I also loved their idea of solving pollution.

In our group project, I worked on the emote redirection feature and submitted a PR. In the process I found the Xcode Simulator has a bad performance and it will freeze when there are too many information to load. I switched to my iPhone for test and it worked much better.
